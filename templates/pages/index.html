How to get a Gif in true sense about your Relocation partner whether he pretends to be a Big Brand

It’s gonna be a decade in which Relocation Industry in India has encountered a terrible rise in the name of Fake Packers & Movers Companies.

Let’s pretend you are a renowned brand name in Relocation Industry and you have a very good presence to your customers, that’s a point where the fake & local immitator starts cheating , make a website

where they try to include some famous name or some elements of the name then advertise ,draw traffic and leads.

For a good customer it is a matter of worry that the name of the Packers and Movers is similar too that of the famous brand they have rolled upon but the Processes and Quality are rarely in existence.

Being a customer it sounds very nice to get a very cheaper price and even more affordable , when the unscrupulous Packers & Movers fail to deliver or cause damage to your articles, you complain them but find no solution , increases unwanted frustration only. It’s something where many of you realise that you have been cheated or tricked by the local moving fellow, By the moment you gonna realize, it’s too late.

Be very cautious while choosing a Moving Company ensure that you are not being grabbed by unprofessional fellows
Here are some essentials you must look for before selecting a relocation agency…


A) Never opt the mover who draws your focus on lower price or quote only
Always remember that relocation industry is a part of transport industry & you must be aware that being a core transport industry it has been treated as a very unorganised sector, hence more than 90% people or vendors are working like a transporter & unprofessional who may be very sweet with you at the time of grabbing booking opportunity whereas only 4-5 % companies are working like a professional Relocation companies in India

Moreover relocation is not necessarily cheap , opting for cheap movers to save money isn’t a wise idea .

Your mover might promise to you at every single thing you want to be done at your way from him but he may be totally different at the time of performance ,remember that you will finally get what you pay for. “ Poor Packing , Low density packaging material , untrained crews as they may hire labour from labour chowk , , fake commitments, hide & seek communication, un-ethical practise , and inexperience in logistics , form a deadly cocktail. You would finally end up with delays , loss of your precious articles , and damaged items.

“ When you add the price of all this to your moving costs you will get the true costs of your home shifting. You would be wise to at least consider this aspect before you choose the mover.”

"Quality is remembered long after even price is forgotten"

B) Look for reviews online
Remember choosing a mover means you are going to handover all your precious & personal house-hold effects that brings dependancy of their sincere responsiveness whether you have chosen a right mover or grabbed by a fake one.

Hence always look for reviews and take some testimonials from some reputed organisations peers or your known for whom they have provided their relocation services earlier.

Aside this don’t just settle for online reviews only as this could be doctored , insist on some customer references from the mover.

Speak up with the customers and ask them about their moving experience , also ask for some packing photographs and videos for the same.

C) Look for the accreditations
Accreditations ensure that the movers adhere to acceptable .ethical standards, in the moving industry such as the movers is accredited by ISO certifications or not that brings some credibility to the clients

D) Ask for Insurance Protection
Most packers and movers do offer insurance as part of the deal but don’t assume this is always the case , also ask what is the procedure for Claim settlement if any damage occurs and what document they are proceeding.

If your movers don’t, always insist on insurance. You don’t want to be left helpless if your goods do get damaged, or stolen in transit, do you?

E) Read the fine print ( T & C ) in the contract carefully
This might sound obvious, but this is one of the biggest mistake most customers make. Read the contract carefully before you sign it. Make sure everything the movers promise is written in the contract. Double check all the dates, insurance details, and the final amount that you have to pay.

Doing this will save you a lot of grief later.
